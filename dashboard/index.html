Information We Collect

The CyberSentinel UEBA platform processes the following categories of data from your environment:

  • User activity logs and behavioural telemetry from monitored endpoints
  • Authentication events, logon/logoff records, and access patterns
  • Network connection metadata (source IP, destination, protocol)
  • Process execution and file access events from agents
  • Dashboard administrator account information
How We Use Your Data

Data collected by the platform is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Detecting anomalous and potentially malicious user behaviour
  • Generating risk scores, verdicts, and security alerts
  • Producing audit trails and compliance reports
  • Improving detection algorithms and platform performance

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
